ABOUT US
A   national   seminar  and  workshop  on  "Rcent Development in the Applied Biotechonology-RDAB-2020" will be held at Indian Institute of Information Techonology, Allahabad, Uttar Pradesh, India, during March 6 to 8, 2020. The primary goal of this programme is to showcase the recent progress in Applied and basic Biotechnology at global level. This will provide a scope to recent advancement of research in the field of Biotechnology which indeed offers several new prospects and opportunities for human healthcare. In addition, participants will get hand on training on the advanced techniques like flourescent activated cell sorters(FACS) and isothernal titration calorimetry(ITC).
